Personal Statement

Early-career researcher specializing in multimodal discourse analysis, with a focus on memory, urbanism, and cultural identity. In my master’s dissertation, I analyzed the Aos Heróis do Ultramar monument in Coimbra by examining the artwork itself, public discourses surrounding it, and the institutionalized discourses within educational institutions. My research combines data analysis and qualitative approaches to explore how monuments and public memory shape collective identity, contributing to the fields of memory studies and urban sociology.
